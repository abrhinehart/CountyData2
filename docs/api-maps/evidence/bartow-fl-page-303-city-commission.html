City Commission

Meetings

  • 6 pm
  • 1st and 3rd Mondays of each month* except when that Monday is a holiday (then the Commission meets on Tuesday)
  • *In September, the first meeting occurs on the first Wednesday and the second meeting is held on the third Tuesday.
  • Bartow City Hall City Commission Chambers
    450 N Wilson Avenue
    Bartow, Florida 33830

Agendas & Minutes

Agendas are available prior to the meetings. Minutes are available following approval.
