Overview

Winter Haven consists of five City Commissioners, which includes an appointed Mayor and Mayor Pro Tem. 

Member Requirements

  • City Commissioners must live within the Winter Haven city limits.
  • Commissioners are elected at large, via a non-partisan election, and serve a 4-year term.
  • The Mayor presides at City Commission Meetings.
  • The Mayor Pro Tem presides in the Mayor’s absence.

City Commission Meeting Recordings

To view recordings from March 2020 to December 2022, visit the City of Winter Haven Facebook Page.

To view recordings from January 2023 to the present, visit the City of Winter Haven YouTube Channel.

  1. Brian Yates

    Phone: 863-662-9131

    William Brian Yates was elected to Seat Number 3 in 2019 and re-elected in 2023. He is serving as the Mayor in 2026. He served as Mayor Pro Tem in 2025. A Winter Haven native and 7th generation Floridian, Brian graduated from Winter Haven High School, attended Polk State College, and graduated from the Florida School of Banking. Brian is a Senior Vice President and the Director of Small Business Lending at Citizens Bank and Trust. He is a member and past President of the Rotary Club of Winter Haven and a past member of the Florence Villa Community Redevelopment Agency (CRA) Advisory Committee. Brian and his wife Samantha have two sons.

    Brian is a board member of the Community Redevelopment Agency (CRA) Board, the Florida League of Cities (FLC) Board of Directors, and serves as the Commission liaison to:

    • FLC Federal Action Strike Team (FAST)
    • FLC Legislative Policy Finance and Taxation Committee 
    • Main Street Winter Haven (MSWH)
    • Planning Commission
    • Polk Transportation Planning Organization (TPO) (Seat Number 2)
    • Ridge League of Cities (RLC) (President Elect) 
    • Polk County Tourist Development Council (TDC)

    tmercer@mywinterhaven.com
    Phone: 863-289-2944

     Lucretia Tracy Mercer was elected to Seat Number 1 in 2019 and re-elected in 2023. Prior to being elected to the City Commission, Tracy was a Commissioner for the Lake Region Lakes Management District. She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Operations from Warner University and an MBA from Webster University in Business Administration. She is a member and past President of the Kiwanis Club of Winter Haven. She is a past president of Keep Winter Haven Clean and Beautiful and a former member of the Garden Club. Tracy has one grown son, one granddaughter and one great-grandson.

  4. Chad Davis

    Commissioner
    cadavis@mywinterhaven.com
    Phone: 863-291-5600 Ext. 2101

    Charles “Chad” Aaron Davis III was elected to Seat Number 4 in 2025. A fourth generation Winter Haven native, Chad graduated from Winter Haven High School and earned a Bachelor of Science in Music Liberal Arts with dual minors in Piano Performance and Philosophy at Trevecca Nazarene University, a Master of Public Policy at Pepperdine University School of Public Policy, and a Juris Doctorate with a Certificate in International and Comparative Law at Pepperdine University Caruso School of Law.
