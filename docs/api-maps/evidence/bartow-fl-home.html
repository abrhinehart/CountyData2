MyBartow App

The City of Bartow has launched a MyBartow app which uses SeeClickFix, a new tool that empowers residents to report quality-of-life issues and request government services. The app is available for both Apple and Android devices from the App Store or on Google Play. Residents also can access the SeeClickFix service from the City’s website. Both the app and the SeeClickFix webpage allow individuals to make service requests with photos, location information, and other details directly to the City’s Customer Service department.
