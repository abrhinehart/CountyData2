Contractor Account Activation

PLEASE READ TO ACTIVATE YOUR ACCOUNT: 1) The name of your company should appear in a drop down box as you type it. 2) Do not use the dash in the Federal Tax ID number. 3) Do not use special characters ($,@,&,#, etc..) in your user name or password. 4) Your password must be 7 digits long and must contain numbers & letters. 5) Only 1 email address can be associated with the account at a time.
