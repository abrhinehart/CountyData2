Frostproof, Florida

The City of Frostproof is conveniently located in Central Florida within an hour’s drive to LEGOLAND, Walt Disney World, the Sebring Raceway, and many other Florida attractions.

Frostproof’s unique heritage and charm are qualities that many find irresistible.  It is not hard to find someone that has moved to Frostproof following a visit to family or friends.

Whether you are a retiree or young couple with a new family, Frostproof is the place for you!

As a sportsman you will find many activities to enjoy.  Some of the nation’s best fishing lakes are located in the Frostproof area, great golf courses, nature trails, state parks, are just a few of the many outdoor opportunities.

Mission Statement and Vision

What happens in Frostproof makes you want to stay in Frostproof!

Rebuild Florida – Housing Repair & Replacement Program

The Rebuild Florida Housing Repair and Replacement Program is a state program launched with federal funding to repair, rebuild or replace eligible homes with remaining damage from Hurricane Ian. For more information, visit Ian.RebuildFlorida.gov

FloridaCommerce has created a toolkit of resources for you to help homeowners navigate the Rebuild Florida Program below:

•            Application Document Checklist

•            Common CDBG-DR Definitions and Acronyms

•            Frequently Asked Questions

•            Inspection Requirements

•            Rebuild Florida Center Locations image

•            Steps to Recovery – flyer and high-level images

•            Step by Step Application User Guide

Encouraging homeowners who meet these qualifications to apply now is important as the Rebuild Florida Program is available to repair damage to their home, replace their mobile home, or reconstruct their stick-built home. Reaching every eligible homeowner in your community is our goal.

To help serve impacted homeowners, we have established seven Rebuild Florida Center Locations to serve residents in person. Additionally, we will launch mobile intake events and outreach events in your community to engage local citizens. Please visit our website Ian.RebuildFlorida.gov for our event schedule and as a resource for your constituents. We have also created a Step-by-Step Application Guide to walk applicants, or those assisting applicants, through the process.

Prioritization is provided to applicants who are low-to-moderate income (below 80% of the area median income and meet one of the following criteria: households that include seniors over the age of 62, children, and/or individuals with disabilities) according to the HUD income limits.

The contractors assisting us with this program are Horne, KPMG, and Indelible Solutions. You will hear from them during the outreach process. Our team is here to help you get Floridians on the path to long-term resiliency, please do not hesitate to reach out.

The City of Frostproof is offering paperless billing and automatic payments – ACH. You can now conveniently receive your water bill by email whether you are home or away. You can also eliminate check writing, postage fees and avoid late fees by signing up for our automatic payment program. – Call City Hall at 863 635-7855 for information.
PAPERLESS BILLING: To enroll, you may pick up a form at Frostproof City Hall or the Latt Maxcy Memorial Library. Fill out the form and return it to City Hall. Enrolled participants will receive their monthly water bill by email.
AUTOMATIC PAYMENTS – ACH: The City provides a convenient program that makes it possible to pay your utility bill without writing a check or making a trip to City Hall. To enroll, you may pick up a form at Frostproof City Hall or the Latt Maxcy Memorial Library. We will automatically deduct the amount of your utility bill each month from your checking account at no charge. You will receive your bill each month for review. The bank draft will process on the 15th of every month.
