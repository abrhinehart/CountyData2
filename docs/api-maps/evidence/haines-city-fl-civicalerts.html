  • Library Artist Demonstration

    Celebrate Earth Day with Florida artist Alice Absolutely at the Haines City Public Library on April 22, 2026 at 11:00 AM for a live artist talk and paint carving demonstration from her Florida Field Guide series.
    Alice will demonstrate her unique carved paint process while discussing how observation of Florida’s native wildlife and landscapes informs her work. The program explores the connection between art, nature, and the changing environment through the lens of her Florida Field Guide project.
    The event will conclude with a question and answer session with the artist.
    This program is designed for adult audiences.
    Supported in part by an Arts & Culture Impact Grant from the Polk Arts & Culture Alliance.
    Haines City Public Library
    111 6th Street, Haines City, FL
    April 22, 2026 | 11:00 AM
