  UPDATE: BURN BAN SUSPENSION EXTENDED UNTIL 4/24

Polk County has extended the countywide burn ban suspension until 11:59 p.m. on April 24, 2026. The burn ban was suspended on April 9, 2026. For more information visit https://www.polkfl.gov/…/polk-county-extends…/

This extension was made by Polk County Fire Chief Shawn Smith, who stated that the decision was based on continuous monitoring of the drought conditions in the area. Residents are encouraged to follow burn safety practices while enjoying recreational fires.

Dear families,

Polk County Public Schools is once again offering a free summer program specifically designed for incoming kindergartners.

Camp Early Bird will be held from June 3 – June 25. This free program is being hosted at specific elementary schools in Polk County.

The summer camp is available to incoming kindergarten students enrolling in a PCPS elementary school. Our experienced, certified teachers will use fun activities and learning experiences to prepare these soon-to-be kindergartners for success in the classroom and beyond.

Students must have their own transportation to the camp. Breakfast and lunch will be provided free of charge for all students.

Parents must register their children online. Registration will open on Wednesday, April 15, at 8 a.m. and close on April 29.

Seats are available on a first-come, first-served basis, so make sure to apply early!

Please visit www.polkschoolsfl.com/earlybird for more information and to access the online registration form.

ANNOUNCEMENT – DUKE ENERGY

As part of Duke Energy’s continuing commitment to provide safe and reliable electrical service to its
communities, it must invest in the proper care and maintenance of its transmission infrastructure.
Part of this maintenance program involves the vegetation management within their right-of-way
corridor. To ensure continued reliable electric service, crews will be pruning and, in some
cases, removing trees within the ICLW-1 69kV transmission power line corridor. Affected landowners
will be notified individually as the project proceeds and prior to work commencing on their property.
Although they do not anticipate any power interruptions at this time, should an outage become
necessary to complete this work, they will notify affected customers in advance. This work is
scheduled to begin in the next few weeks. This notice constitutes the notice required by Florida
Statute 163.3209.

Public Utilities Notices

      Modified Phase 3 Extreme Water Shortage Restrictions

Southwest Florida Water Management District’s (District) Governing Board to declare a Modified Phase III “Extreme” Water Shortage due to ongoing drought conditions. All Residents including those on private wells are to follow one-day-per-week watering restrictions with new hours effective are effective from April 3, 2026, through July 1, 2026.

Beginning April 3rd, water hours are reduced to Before 4 a.m. or After 8 p.m. Watering days are as follows: Even addresses can water on Tuesdays and Odd addresses can water on Thursdays. Properties less than one (1) acre in size may only use one of these windows. Properties over one (1) acre may only water before 4am or after 8pm.

Let’s all do our part to protect our water supply!  See additional information below!_______________________________________________________________________________________

We’re Looking for Community-Minded Volunteers!

The Town of Dundee is currently seeking dedicated residents to Serve on the Dundee Tree Board!

The Tree Board plays an important role in helping shape a greener, more beautiful Dundee. Members work together to guide tree planting and preservation efforts, promote proper tree care, and support community events that highlight the value of our urban forest.

From enhancing public spaces to educating residents on the importance of trees, the Tree Board helps keep our town healthy, vibrant, and sustainable for years to come. Interested in making a difference? We’d love to have you join us! Please contact the Melissa Glogowski, in the Town Clerks office at 863.438.8330 ext. 271 or MGlogowski@townofdundee.com to request a board application.
