welcome to Fort Meade

Fort Meade is the oldest city in Polk County, Florida, United States. The population was 5,626 at the 2010 census. As of 2018, the population recorded by the U.S. Census Bureau is 6,203. It is part of the Lakeland–Winter Haven Metropolitan Statistical Area.
