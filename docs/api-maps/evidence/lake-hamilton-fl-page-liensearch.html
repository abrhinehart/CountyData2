Lien Search Request

The Town of Lake Hamilton requires an application and fee to perform lien search requests. When requesting information relating to utilities, code violations, and open and expired permits, a lien search may take up to five business days to complete. 

Payments must be accompanied with a lien search request form, otherwise, the town will not be able to process the request. 

For questions or address verification, please call the Permit Coordinator at 863-439-1910.

Under Florida law, email addresses are public records. If you do not want your email address released in response to a public records request, do not send electronic mail to this entity. Instead, contact this office by phone or in writing.
