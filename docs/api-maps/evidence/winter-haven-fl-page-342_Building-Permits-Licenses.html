Business Tax Receipts

The Building Division issues Business Tax Receipts to all businesses and organizations within the city limits of Winter Haven.

The Business Tax Receipt renewal fees are due October 1st every year.

Prior to starting a Business please check the following: 

  • Zoning Requirements
  • Fire Protection Requirements
  • License / Certification Requirements

Please complete the application and submit it with all the supporting documents to the Business Tax Specialist.

  • Peddlers Business Tax Receipt Application (PDF)

Building Permit Inspection Requests

For 2025 and older permits: Email BuildingInspections@myWinterHaven.com and supply the following information in the BODY of the email:

  1. Permit Number (Starts with the year) i.e. 25-
  2.  Site Address
  3. Type of Inspection
  4. Contact Number
  5. Date of inspection if not for the next day
  6. Gate Codes

For 2026 permits and newer please use the Accela Portal to schedule inspections or text inspection requests to the phone number 888-513-8552.

Inspections Codes & Inspections

Text to Schedule Inspections

Requests must be made by 7 am for same day inspections.

For Fire Inspections, please allow 48 hours for inspection to be scheduled.

All required documentation (approved plans, truss engineering, permit placard, etc.) must be on the job site, accessible to the inspector, and protected from the weather. Any deviation may result in work delays and/or re-inspection fees imposed.
